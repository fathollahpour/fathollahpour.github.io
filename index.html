About Me

I am a second year M.Sc. student in CS at McMaster University‬. I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Hassan Ashtiani‬. I received my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Amirkabir University of Technology in 2019.

Research Interests

My research interests mostly lie at the intersection of theoretical computer science and machine learning. More specifically, I am interested in the theory of learning and studying the capacity of neural networks. I also have an interest in proving generalization guarantees for neural networks under noisy settings.
