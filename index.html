About Me

I am a machine learning researcher in the department of Computing and Software, McMaster University supervised by Dr. Hassan Ashtiani‬. I received my M.Sc. degree in CS from McMaster University‬ in 2022 where I was fortunate enough to be advised by Dr. Hassan Ashtiani‬. I received my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Amirkabir University of Technology in 2019.

Research Interests

My research interests mostly lie at the intersection of machine learning, algorithms, and statistics. I have a specific interest in analyzing the efficiency of learning algorithms and studying their generalization and robustness. I want to know how well the theoretical results can describe learning algorithms in practice. Particularly, I am in interested in initiating tools to analyze the efficiency of algorithms and to offer insights for designing them.
